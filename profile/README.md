## 👋 About us

**Heudiasyc** (*Heuristics and Diagnosis of Complex Systems*) is a joint research lab of [CNRS](https://www.cnrs.fr/) and [Université de technologie de Compiègne (UTC)](https://www.utc.fr/), founded in **1981**. We work on the science and engineering of intelligent, autonomous, and trustworthy systems — spanning **computer science, control theory, robotics, and artificial intelligence**.

Our research covers **autonomous and connected vehicles, mobile robotics and drones, machine learning, uncertainty quantification, and dependability/security for complex systems** — from fundamental research to applied work with industrial partners, including the joint lab SIVALab with Renault. This repository hub is where that research becomes open, reproducible code for the wider robotics and AI community.

## 🏗️ What we build it with

A glimpse of the experimental platforms behind the repos in this org:

- 🚗 **Intelligent vehicles** — a fleet of instrumented vehicles dedicated to developing and integrating technology building blocks for driving assistance
- 🛸 **Robotics** — a platform of small mobile robots and aerial drones (mini-drones)
- 🕶️ **Immersive simulation** — a 4-wall CAVE plus an autonomous-driving and railway-supervision simulator
- 🤝 **SIVALab** — joint lab with Renault (CNRS/UTC/Renault) on localization and perception systems for autonomous vehicles, est. 2017
